Seasonal Destination
- Spring -

Are you searching for a perfect place to visit in Spring?
Here are our recommendations for you. If you are interested in visiting some of the places, please let us know. We are happy to recommend a suitable itinerary for you.

Yeouido cherry blossom

Period: 5 April ~ 30 April (this period is subject to change)
Location: Seoul

Enjoy Spectacular Spring Blossoms here in Yeouido. More than 1,600 trees of Jeju origin will bloom along the Han River basked in spring fragrance, in addition to nearly 90,000 flowers including azaleas, forsythias, and spireas.

Jinhae Gunhangje Festival

Period: 1 April ~ 10 April (this period is subject to change)
Location: Jinhae

Let's enjoy the world's largest cherry blossom festival in the military port city! In particular, 'Gunakjang Festival' that embodies the characteristics of the military port city is a military art performance that combines the chairman and the military music, and a powerful masquerade performance of the military band.

Jeju rape flower

Period: (this period is subject to change)
Location: Jeju

The blue seas of Jeju and dark stone walls in the streets are set off by the yellow flowers and the place is popular with couples and families. Don't miss the great views!

Everland Tulip festival

Period: 26 March ~ 30 April (this period is subject to change)
Location: Gyeonggi

Everland is filled with 1.2 million spring flowers including tulips, daffodils, and grape hyacinths. The garden features sculptures and paths decorated with colorful spring flowers.

Everland rose festival

Period: 14 May ~ 13 June (this period is subject to change)
Location: Gyeonggi

For over three decades, Everland has been hosting the annual Rose Festival to celebrate the beloved flower. You will be able to enjoy the amusement park decorated with a beautiful rose.
