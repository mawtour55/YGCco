    Tourist attraction information

    • Strawberry farm: The farm is well kept for children to have a strawberry picking experience. You will also learn about how strawberries grow which will make the picking experience more fun. You will enjoy the sweet juicy strawberry.
    • Pocheon art valley: Oddly shaped rocks and a beautiful lake were once a closed quarry. The transformation was the outcome of Korea's first environment restoration project led by Pocheon-si. As of now, more than 1 million tourists visit this place every year.
    • Hantan river Sky bridge: Also known for the filming location for Crash Landing on You, the Hantan river Sky bridge is 200 meters long and allows 1,500 adults (80kg) to pass at the same time, and offers a 50m view of the Hantan River Canyon. You will find a glass floor in the middle of the Sky bridge. Let's take a deep breath and cross the bridge!
    • Bidulginang Waterfalls: In the old times, pigeons, or bidulgi in Korean, lived inside the cave behind the waterfall over a hollow cliff that resembled a pigeon’s nest, which is how the name “Bidulginang Waterfalls” originated. The waterfall is surrounded by grand columnar joints. The landscape appears quite mysterious and serene, earning it a spot as a popular filming site for historical dramas. It was also a shooting place for Netflix drama Kingdom.
